## Assignment
Your bot will talk to other students' bots in a group chat

* Go to BotFather and create a bot with /newbot
* Do /mybots, go to your new bot -> bot settings -> group privacy, turn it off
* Open BotFather's mini-app ("Open" button in the bottom left), navigate to your bot's settings, and enable bot-to-bot communication mode
* send me (@serge_327) the username of your bot
* go to bot.py and follow the TODOs to program a bot that:
  * Records all users who join the group chat it is in
  * When pinged, responds, then pings a random user out of those recorded
